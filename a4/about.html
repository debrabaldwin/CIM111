The Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour


The Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour is a Miami-beach resort that defines exceptional, each day. One of the most sought-after 5-star hotels Miami offers, it’s ideally located in the exclusive Bal Harbour Village neighborhood, where boutique shopping, superlative restaurants and a vibrant art scene thrive. Renowned as one of the finest Miami wedding venues, the Ritz Carlton Bal Harbour blends the elegance of elevated service with captivating amenities. Nestled along the pristine stretches of sand and ocean that have been drawing visitors for decades, it’s a destination that defines the best of beach resorts. Miami, with its vibrancy is minutes away, yet the service and style of our property inspires welcome relaxation.From our ocean-to-table cuisine, to our indulgent spa and European-edged outdoor heated pool, The Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour is a destination as unique as its location.
